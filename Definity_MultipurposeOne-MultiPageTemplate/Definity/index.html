Our Mission

The skillbased platform

At the heart of our mission is a simple yet powerful goal: to seamlessly connect skilled consultants with clients seeking specific expertise. We understand that finding the right match can transform businesses and drive success. Our platform is designed with precision, offering the essential tools and functionalities to facilitate this crucial connection.

For consultants, we provide a space to showcase their unique competencies, reach a broader audience, and access opportunities that align with their skills and passions. For clients, we ensure a streamlined process to find the ideal consultants that can meet their specific needs, driving projects to successful completion with precision and efficiency.

Our platform is more than just a meeting point; it is a dynamic ecosystem where expertise and opportunities converge. Together, we are building a future where the right connections empower growth, innovation, and success for all.

Our Story

Consulting and creating a family-company

As consultants, we often struggle to find job opportunities that truly match and leverage our skills and experience. Projects always require a unique combination of specific expertise through its various phases, and finding the right resources is a very critical but time-consuming task.

Having had this experience again and again prompted the need for a better solution. We envisioned a platform where consultants could easily showcase their competencies and clients could quickly find the perfect match for their needs.

Many years in the making, in parallell to working as a consultant, the small family-based Skillbased team, have created a platform to streamline the connection process, making it easier for consultants to find the right opportunities and for clients to access specialized expertise efficiently.

Kristian Veem

FOUNDER & CEO

How it got started...

"Balancing full-time jobs as a consultant and a side project was never easy, but the vision for a dedicated consulting platform kept me going. Having worked with business transformation projects all my career, I saw the gap that needed to be filled. My team and lead developer Marcin have poured our energy into building this platform together, driven by the desire to create a skill-based network."

"What started as a side project I juggled alongside raising three kids has now evolved. My sons are grown and have stepped into the business, contributing with their unique skills and enthusiasm. Today, we work together, turning the side project into a thriving family endeavor."
