Qualify as a SkillBased consultant

tessttestes
The majority of consultants are working in niche firms with five or fewer employees. These are professionals with expert skills, operational execution experience from specific industries and functional areas. When you are small it is difficult to market your services and be found outside your network.

  • Make sure our clients find you through your skills and competence.
  • By working in extended networks, you can leverage your skills and keep building experience.
  • With operational experience, you can support clients implement change and reach targets.
  • Perfect matching gives efficiency in delivery, rather than price pressure.
  • Flexible business models enables you to expand your proposition and find complementary resources when you own the client relationship.
Just as we work with developing our clients processes, we strive to make the resourcing process more efficient for all parties in the market. That means flexibility and cooperation in partnerships and business models, to enable value added solutions to clients. It also means using digital profiling tools and processes that build matching solutions focusing on skills and relevant experience.

We offer you a free consultant profiling tool, that you can use for all purposes of presenting yourself and your competences. The more detailed and elaborate you are, the better matches you can get on assignments that will leverage your skillset.

Right now we are focusing on builing the competence network for clients in Financial Services, Banking and Insurance. If this is your area of expertise, please register to become part of our network to get included in our search and matching.
