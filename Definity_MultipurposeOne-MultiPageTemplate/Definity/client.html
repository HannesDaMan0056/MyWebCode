SkillBased Consulting service model

Your ability to deliver complex change projects according to goals and within targets is greatly enhanced when you are working with the right resources. Skillbased Consulting is a resourcing partner for businesses and organizations to find the most relevant skills available when the need for external support and expert advice arises.

We work closely with you to get a thorough understanding of your vision, goals, prerequisites and requirements. You will be assigned a senior consultant that has expert industry experience to advise and guide you throughout this process and service delivery.

Just as we work with developing our clients processes, we strive to make the resourcing process more efficient for all parties in the market. That means flexibility and cooperation in partnerships and business models, to enable value added solutions to clients. It also means using digital profiling tools and processes to proactively expand our network, to give quality matching of skills and relevant experience.

Whatever specific requirements you have in your situation, we put our effort in finding the consultants that fill your resource gaps and competence needs. Our mission is to deliver the most relevant skills based on your needs and requirements.

Skillbased Consulting positioning:

  • Majority of consultants are self-employed or work in firms with five or fewer employees. They are experts with experience from operational execution. Our priority is to build a network to find these experts and know their specialty skills.
  • External support is invaluable in change projects where you are moving into the unknown, so by adding resources with experience from doing just what you are about to do, it gives guidance and insights in how to succeed.
  • Large consulting firms prioritize using their employed resources. Skillbased Consulting are resource brokers focusing on finding the right competence regardless of where they are employed.
  • Sometimes you need a reputable firm name as a sort of insurance policy. But in change projects, when achieving your goals on time and budget is more important, working with the right resources will be a better insurance for reaching targets.

Skillbased consultants will:

  • Manage roles such as; advisor, program/project manager, business analyst, specialist, or interim manager.
  • Have experience from operational execution and implementation.
  • Bring valuable insights on how to succeed and what to avoid.
  • Provide complementary skills and perspectives to your own resources.
  • Be efficient and productive from start.
  • Compete with their skills, expertise and ability to understand your needs.
  • Leverage their skills and build their expertise to make you succeed.
  • Be found even if you are outside each other's network.
