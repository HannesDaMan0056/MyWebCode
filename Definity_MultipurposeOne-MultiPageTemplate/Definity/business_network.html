Start working with our consultant tool

Take control of your consultant profile, expand your network & be found when your skills are requested
  • Your career history Stored & managed in one place
  • Search & Match Matching skills to client needs
  • Expand your network Be relevant when it's relevant

Control your story

Build and control your career history, in one place, where you always keep it updated. Get started easily by importing your LinkedIn profile and add additional details and skills to it. Add work experiences and projects and tag them with specific skills used and acquired.

Use this structured database as the single source for all your applications and profiles/CVs. Create unlimited number of customized profiles for each role or assignment and control how the links are distributed.

Tagging & rating skills

By adding and rating skills to each project or position, you increase search matching accuracy. When your skills and experiences are requested, you can customize a profile that displays your skills and competences.

When adding industry, functional area and career level it brings additional accuracy to how your skills are interpreted. In addition, you rate your skill knowledge level and number of years you have practiced it.

Market your expertise

As a freelance you must market yourself, but the market for your skillset is very specific. By working in a network you can be found when your skillset is in demand. More efficient for you and for the clients.

In the consultant tool you can find active assignments that are open and apply with a customized profile. Or you can be found and contacted by search agents that match the client requirements to your skills. In the settings you can configure if you are available, your notice period and preferred rate level.
