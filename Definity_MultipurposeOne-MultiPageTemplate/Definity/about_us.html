About Us / Our People

About us

At Skillbased, our mission is to empower consultants and consultancies with a robust and dynamic platform that enhances their ability to connect, collaborate, and grow. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities within the consulting industry because we are a consultancy firm ourselves. Our goal is to streamline the process of finding the right talent for the right projects, ensuring that both our clients and consultants achieve their objectives efficiently and effectively.

Our vision is to be a trusted consulting tool and network that facilitates meaningful interactions between consultants and clients. We strive to keep evolving and adapting to the changing needs of the industry, combining technology with our consulting experiences. The unique combination of being both a consultancy and a platform provider allows us to understand the challenges and opportunities within consulting from all perspectives. At Skillbased, we value expertise, collaboration, and innovation, and we are committed to supporting our community in achieving great results.

What we do

At Skillbased we opperate in two levels:

  1. Skillbased Platform: Our innovative platform is designed to cater to both individual consultants and consultancy firms. It serves as a comprehensive tool for finding, vetting, and engaging with top-tier consultants across various industries. Whether you are a consultant looking for your next lucrative project or a consultancy seeking specialized expertise, our platform offers a user-friendly interface and powerful search capabilities to meet your needs.

  2. Skillbased Consulting: In addition, we also run a consultancy on our platform that leverages our extensive network to provide bespoke consulting services to our clients. We actively search for the best matches between our clients needs and the consultants in our network, ensuring that each project is staffed with the most qualified professionals. As such, we both build the platform network and our ability to meet client needs.
